A clear loan and amortization tracker. Plan what you borrow or lend, see schedules and charts—and optionally sign in with an account to keep your data in sync.

Open app

What it does

Track loans you take out or give to others. Set amounts, interest rates, and start dates, then add amortization plans (one-off or recurring) to see exactly what you’ll pay and when.

  • Borrowing and lending in one place
  • Interest and loan amount changes over time
  • Amortization table and chart per loan
  • Export and import your data
  • Optional account—sign in on any browser to sync your data
  • Share a loan with someone or transfer ownership

See it in action

Your loans at a glance, with schedules and charts when you open the app.

Get started

No sign-up required to try it: open the app and add a loan. Create an account when you want your data to sync (e.g. on another browser or device) or to share a loan.
